Quick sensory reset

Pick one. 10–60 seconds. Stop if discomfort increases. Not for Red days — on Red, go to the Red protocol instead.
A · PressureCross arms, firm pressure on upper arms or shoulders — or press palms together. Hold 10–30s.
B · Visual reductionClose eyes, lower gaze to a neutral surface, or turn away from stimulation. 10–30s.
C · Auditory dampeningCover ears lightly, hum softly on a long exhale, or press tongue to roof of mouth. 15–45s.
D · Breath anchorInhale normally, exhale slowly 6–8s. Repeat 1–3 times only.
E · Micro-relocationOne step away from the stimulus, turn 90°, or sit if standing / stand if sitting. Pause 10–30s.
After one reset, reassess. Better → stop. Not better → one more attempt, then drop to a lower Day Type protocol.
